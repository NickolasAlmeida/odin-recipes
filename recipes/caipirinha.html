Caipirinha

Brazil's national cocktail is so refreshing and delicious! Ask your liquor store to order Cachaca
for you if they don't stock it, there's no substitute.

    Ingredients:

  • ½ lime, quartered
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 ½ fluid ounces cachaca
  • 1 cup ice cubes

    Instructions:

  1. In a large rocks glass squeeze and drop in 2 eighths of lime. Add sugar, crush and mix with a spoon. Pour in the cachaca and plenty of ice. Stir well.n
